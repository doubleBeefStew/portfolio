My Projects

Welcome to my web playground! Each project here is a little digital adventure I've embarked on. From banking apps to e-commerce sites, these are the pixels I'm most proud of. Take a peek and see what we could build together!

MyBCA - Banking Web Application

MyBCA is one of the most used banking web application in Indonesia. In this project I mainly focus on administrations, transfer, and payment & purchase modules.

Our transfer service provide real-time transaction and is secured with both soft and hard tokens.

Our payment & purchase services provides ease of transaction for users to pay bills and top-up vouchers for various need, such as electricity bills, credit card installment or taxes.

Go ahead and visit this website for a quick demo.

DealDash - E-Commerce Website

In my spare time, I've been nurturing a passion project: a fully-functional e-commerce website. This digital marketplace encompasses all the essential features you'd expect from a modern online shopping platform.

Users can browse and search for products with ease, manage their shopping carts, and complete purchases securely through PayPal integration.

The site also offers a robust order tracking system, allowing customers to follow their purchases from click to delivery.

User engagement is enhanced through email confirmations and comprehensive profile management tools. More features to come as I continuously develop this project as I see it fit :)
